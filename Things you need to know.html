Details

Here's the essential information about yoga classes at Acas. If you have a question that's not answered below, please drop us an email: yoga@acas.org.uk

What am I paying for?

The cost is £60 for a block of six classes. To keep things simple we are asking for a one-off cash payment to cover all six classes. Payment reserves your place. Please give your money to Cate McLaurin or Philippa Newis.

What are the dates?

Classes are on Wednesdays on the following dates:

  • 25th April
  • 2nd May
  • 9th May
  • 16th May
  • 23rd May
  • 30th May

What if I can’t come to a class?

The bad news is that we cannot refund your place. The good news is that we have a waiting list of people who are interested in classes. It is up to you to organise a swap and payment with someone from the list. Get in touch and we can tell you who to approach.

What time does the class start?

Classes start promptly at 5.30pm. Each session lasts an hour.

Do I need to bring a mat?

No. Please let us know if you need a mat and we can provide one.

What do I wear to yoga?

Here’s some suggestions:

  • Wear comfortable clothes that you can easily move in
  • Avoid loose or baggy tops as these can ride up during the class. You may not want to bare all to your colleagues
  • You can do yoga in bare feet or socks
  • Bring some water with you.

What level are the classes?

These are beginner classes. Whether or not you have tried yoga before, everyone is welcome.
