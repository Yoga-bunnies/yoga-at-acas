Meet the teacher

Jacqui Hooper

Originally a professional dancer I made the transition into yoga through Bikram and completed my intensive training over 3 months in LA in 2012. My style is a mix of tai chi & yoga with great emphasis on breathing, slow controlled and graceful movement.
