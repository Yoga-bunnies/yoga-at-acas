Jacqui Hooper

I found yoga when I was at dance school back in Australia, it wasn’t my favourite class I must admit. It’s a love/hate relationship for me, but it does feel damn good and keeps me out of trouble. When it comes to music, If you want om-ing, chanting or whale music, you’ll be disappointed… I love Electronic shark tunes. When I’m not practicing I enjoy eating, drinking, listening to music, the usual suspects but my guilty pleasure is watching the X factor. I live by these words “Be the person my dog thinks I am”.
